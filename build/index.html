  • Virtual Clusters for Community Computation

    A platform for organizing and automating delivery of scientfic computing resources into a single logical environment.

  • User Communities

    Bring your institutional HPC cluster, INCITE/XSEDE allocation, or cloud resource into a single environment, controlled by policies you define.

  • HPC, Cloud and Grid Service Providers

    VC3 will offer new approaches to present your HPC resources to scientific collaborations, large or small, or individual investigators.

  • Workload developers

    Deal with resources in the abstract when you want to, or get more details when you need them. VC3 platforms will host modular services that orchestrate delivery of infrastructure to workload management systems.

VC3 platforms will automate processes that discover, provision, and orchestrate the resources to build virtual clusters—dynamic infrastructures that enable extreme scale scientific computation.

Discover

Create an environment that facilitates the discovery of computing resources like processor time, data storage and networking across geographically distributed, dynamic virtual organizations.

Provision

Allocate resources from existing facilities to virtually provision environments with general guidance from scientists. Provisioning should be flexible, resilient, high throughput, and rapidly reconfigurable at runtime.

Orchestrate

Find ways to streamline data management and analysis. Make research results friendly to interactive analytics and discoverable by current and future team members.
